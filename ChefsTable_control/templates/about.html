About Us

Based in London, United Kingdom ChefsTable is a family owned restaurant, focused mainly on traditional Indian recipes served with a modern twist. With our cuisines expanding from Indian to Itaian, mexican, chinese putting on an Indian twist to each of these cusines.

A 7 star experience costing you as less as food from a food truck. We believe in giving the best experince to all our customer starting with our lounge, to the seating the view and the food. Food doesn't have to be fancy, it just has to be tasty enough so you can get through your day.

The restaurant has several different looks with a rustic and relaxed atmosphere, to a classy modern 7 star hotel environment, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day. You can have a classic cafe environment or a club environment whatever you are in the mood for.

ChefsTable is owned by Nitin who built this restaurant solely for the purpose of showing the world that food can be tasty and affordable by everyone.

ChefsTable is an all vegetarian, non alcoholic restaurant. This was built considering the fact there are not many only vegetarian restaurants in the world. ChefsTable brings you the 7 star experience to an all vegetarian based cusine.
